What is CODE:RUSH

INTECS initiated the coderush competition to find your hidden skills in problem solving and coding. In this overnight hackathon you need to solve challenges as a team which are published in hackerrank platform by our experienced problem setters. These challenges are from ad-hoc, arrays, stack/queue, grahp, string manipulation, bit manipulation, recursion, dynamic programming, and mathematical algorithms categories. This grand opportunity bestowed only to the members of FIT family. So get the most out of this event. Happy Coding.

Problem Solving

This is a great chance to test and polish up your skills in problem solving. Try these cool challenges and make it a chance to know what you are capable of.

Fun

This is a great opportunity to have fun with your friends while competing hand in hand. Beat your friends by coding.

Coding

Make it your habit to code. Grab the chance to code in your favorite programming language and feel the magic in it.

How it works

Assemble a team ,and get registered for CodeRush. Once this is done, you will be assigned with Hackerrank accounts (one per each team) and you will receive further information via emails.
